🏠 Mera Pakistan Mera Ghar — Home Loan Scheme

Government subsidized scheme for first-time home buyers. Category 1 (up to Rs. 2.7M): 2–5% annual rate. Category 2 (Rs. 2.7M–6M): 5–7% annual rate. Tenure up to 20 years.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Can I get a loan without salary slip in Pakistan?+
Self-employed individuals can provide bank statements and tax returns as income proof. Some banks offer loans against assets. Salaried employees with salary slip have the easiest approval process.
Minimum salary for personal loan in Pakistan?+
Most banks require Rs. 25,000–50,000 monthly salary. HBL typically requires Rs. 30,000+ monthly. Exact requirement varies by bank and loan amount requested.
Is Meezan Bank loan halal?+
Yes — Meezan offers Islamic financing (Murabaha, Ijarah) based on Shariah principles rather than traditional interest. It is the most popular choice for Pakistani Muslims seeking halal finance.
How long for loan approval in Pakistan?+
Personal loans: 3–10 working days. Car loans: 5–7 days. Home loans: 2–4 weeks. Having complete documents ready speeds up the process significantly.
