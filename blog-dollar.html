❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Where to check live dollar rate in Pakistan?+
For interbank rate: SBP.org.pk (official). For open market rates: Forex.pk or any sarafa exchange shop. Rates change multiple times daily.
Why is dollar rate different at exchange shops vs banks?+
Banks use SBP's official interbank rate. Exchange shops (sarafa bazaar) use open market rates set by demand and supply. Open market is usually slightly higher than interbank.
How much dollars can I bring to Pakistan legally?+
Any amount. But if carrying more than USD 10,000, you must declare at customs. There is no limit on incoming foreign currency.
Is it better to save in dollars or PKR in Pakistan?+
Historically USD hedges against PKR depreciation. But Pakistani bank savings accounts earning 15–20% interest can also perform well. Depends on your financial goals and time horizon.
