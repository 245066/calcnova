💵 Minimum Wage in Pakistan 2025

📋 Federal Minimum Wage 2025

The federal minimum wage in Pakistan as of 2025 is approximately Rs. 37,000 per month (~USD 133). This is a legal minimum floor — employers cannot pay less.

🤝 How to Negotiate Better Salary in Pakistan

1. Research market rate — Use LinkedIn, Rozee.pk to know what others earn.
2. Get a competing offer — Best leverage in any negotiation.
3. Quantify your value — "I increased sales by 30%" beats "I work hard."
4. Time it right — Ask after a big success or during appraisal season.
5. IT freelancing — Even USD 200–500/month on Upwork/Fiverr can double effective income.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What is a good salary in Pakistan 2025?+
In Karachi/Lahore, Rs. 80,000+ is comfortable for a single person. Rs. 150,000+ allows a comfortable family life. In smaller cities, Rs. 50,000–60,000 is a good salary.
How much tax on Rs. 100,000 salary in Pakistan?+
On Rs. 100,000/month (Rs. 1.2M annual), income tax is approximately Rs. 1,250/month. Net take-home is approximately Rs. 98,750 before EOBI. Use our salary calculator for exact figures.
Is EOBI mandatory in Pakistan?+
Yes for companies with 5+ employees. Employee pays 1% of wages and employer pays 5%. Provides pension benefits upon retirement.
How to earn more money in Pakistan?+
Learn high-demand IT skills (software dev, data science), freelance internationally on Upwork/Fiverr, start an online business, or invest in real estate. Many Pakistanis double their income through part-time freelancing.
