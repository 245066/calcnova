📐 Salary Calculator Formula

Net Salary = Gross − Income Tax − EOBI − Other Deductions
Income tax calculated on annual basis using FBR progressive tax slabs

Pakistan uses a progressive income tax system where higher earners pay a higher percentage. Tax is first calculated on annual income, then divided by 12 for monthly deduction. Salaries up to PKR 50,000/month (Rs. 600,000 annual) are completely tax-free.

📋 How to Calculate Salary Calculator Manually

  • 1
    Multiply your monthly gross salary by 12 to get annual income.
  • 2
    Find which FBR tax slab your annual income falls in (see table below).
  • 3
    Calculate the annual tax using the slab formula, then divide by 12 for monthly tax.
  • 4
    Subtract EOBI (1%) if applicable — mandatory for most formal sector employees.
  • 5
    The remaining amount is your estimated net take-home salary.

📊 Calculation Example

📋 Example — Rs. 80,000 Monthly Salary

Annual income: 80,000 × 12 = Rs. 960,000

Tax slab: 600,001–1,200,000 (2.5% on excess over 600,000)

Annual tax: (960,000 − 600,000) × 2.5% = 360,000 × 0.025 = Rs. 9,000

Monthly tax: 9,000 ÷ 12 = Rs. 750

EOBI: 80,000 × 1% = Rs. 800

Net Salary: 80,000 − 750 − 800 = Rs. 78,450

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What is the income tax on Rs. 100,000 salary in Pakistan?+
On Rs. 100,000/month (Rs. 1,200,000 annual), income tax is (1,200,000-600,000)×2.5%÷12 = Rs. 1,250/month. Net salary after tax and EOBI is approximately Rs. 97,750.
Is EOBI mandatory in Pakistan?+
Yes, EOBI (Employees' Old-Age Benefits Institution) is mandatory for all companies with 5 or more employees. The employee pays 1% of basic wages and the employer pays 5%. It provides pension benefits after retirement.
What is the minimum tax-free salary in Pakistan 2025?+
Annual income up to Rs. 600,000 (Rs. 50,000 per month) is completely tax-free in Pakistan under FBR 2024-25 budget. No income tax is deducted on salaries at or below this threshold.
How to calculate take-home salary in Pakistan?+
Take-home = Gross Salary − Income Tax − EOBI (1%) − any other deductions. Use our salary calculator above for instant and accurate calculation based on FBR 2024-25 tax slabs.
Does Pakistan have professional tax?+
Yes, some provinces charge Professional Tax ranging from Rs. 200-1,200/year depending on salary level and province (Punjab, Sindh, KPK, Balochistan). It is typically deducted monthly from salary.
