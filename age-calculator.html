📐 Age Calculator Formula

Age = Current Date − Date of Birth
Calculated in years, months and remaining days

Age calculation seems simple but requires careful handling of months with different numbers of days, leap years, and the exact day difference. The formula involves subtracting the birth date from the current date while accounting for calendar irregularities.

The most accurate method is to calculate year difference first, then adjust for months, and finally adjust for remaining days. This is exactly how our age calculator works.

📋 How to Calculate Age Calculator Manually

  • 1
    Note today's date and your date of birth (day, month, year).
  • 2
    Subtract the birth year from current year to get approximate age in years.
  • 3
    Check if your birthday has already passed this year. If not, subtract 1 from the year count.
  • 4
    Calculate the remaining months by subtracting birth month from current month.
  • 5
    Calculate the remaining days by subtracting birth day from current day, adjusting for month lengths.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I calculate my exact age?+
To calculate exact age, subtract your birth date from today's date. Count full years first, then remaining months, then remaining days. Our age calculator does this automatically and also shows total days, weeks and hours lived.
What is my age if I was born in 2000?+
If you were born in 2000, you are 25 or 26 years old in 2026 depending on whether your birthday has passed this year. Use our age calculator above for the exact result.
How many days old am I?+
To find how many days old you are, multiply your age in years by 365 and add days from partial years. Our calculator shows exact days lived. For example, a 25-year-old is approximately 9,125 days old.
Can I calculate age between two specific dates?+
Yes! Our age calculator has a 'Calculate Age On' field where you can enter any date. This lets you calculate age at a past or future date, or find the age difference between two people.
What is the formula for age in days?+
Days of age = (Current date - Birth date) in milliseconds ÷ 86,400,000 (milliseconds per day). Our calculator uses this precise formula for accurate results.
