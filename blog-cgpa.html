How to Calculate CGPA in Pakistan — University Grade System Guide 2025

📅 Updated: 2025⏱ 7 min read🎓 Education🔍 2,900+ monthly searches
CGPA (Cumulative Grade Point Average) is the most important number for Pakistani university students. Whether you are at NUST, FAST, UET, COMSATS, or any HEC-recognized university, this guide explains the grading system, how to calculate CGPA accurately, and how to improve your academic standing.

🔢 CGPA Calculation Formula

📐 Formula

CGPA = Sum of (Grade Points × Credit Hours) ÷ Total Credit Hours

Example: Math 3cr B+(3.3)=9.9, Physics 3cr A-(3.7)=11.1, English 2cr B(3.0)=6.0
CGPA = (9.9+11.1+6.0)÷8 = 27÷8 = 3.375

HEC formula: Percentage = (CGPA ÷ 4.0) × 100

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What is a good CGPA in Pakistan?+
CGPA 3.0+ is generally good. For HEC scholarships and top companies, 3.5+ is ideal. Many tech companies accept 2.5+ CGPA for fresh graduates with strong skills.
How to convert CGPA to percentage?+
Formula: Percentage = (CGPA / 4.0) x 100. So CGPA 3.5 = 87.5%. Always mention the scale (out of 4.0) when submitting to employers or HEC.
Does CGPA matter for jobs in Pakistan?+
Yes for fresh graduates, especially large companies and government jobs. After 2–3 years of experience, skills and portfolio matter much more than CGPA in most Pakistani industries.
Can I improve CGPA in final year?+
Yes but limited impact. Focus on A grades in all remaining courses. Check if your university allows retaking previous courses for grade replacement.
