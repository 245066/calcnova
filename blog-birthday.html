❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Unique birthday gift under Rs. 2000 in Pakistan?+
A customized mug or photo frame with a meaningful photo, a quality chocolate gift box, a nice journal with pen set, or a scented candle with bath products. Personalization makes small gifts memorable.
Can I get birthday gifts delivered online in Pakistan?+
Yes. Daraz.pk delivers nationwide. For same-day flowers and cake delivery in Karachi, Lahore, Islamabad try FNP Pakistan (fnp.com/pk). Many local bakeries also do same-day cake delivery.
Best birthday gift for Pakistani mother?+
High-quality prayer mat with tasbeeh, gold earrings or small bangle, beautiful shalwar kameez in her favourite colour, or a family photo album. For modern mothers, a spa day or beauty treatment is wonderful.
Are gift cards available in Pakistan?+
Yes — Daraz vouchers, Outfitters, Khaadi gift cards are available. Amazon gift cards (for overseas use) are also popular among younger Pakistanis.
